Experience the pleasure and satisfaction of creating your own hand drum that is as unique as you.  It will be an original work of art deeply connected to your spirit, which can be enjoyed for many years to come.

Let the organizers know of your interest by completing the survey form below.
The drum is a sacred instrument that can be used for grounding, clearing and journeying.  The voice and rhythm of the drum can connect us with our heart and the earth...
You will build a traditional hand drum, crafted using a wooden hoop frame and a stretched rawhide head.  Covered only on one side, the back of the drum is laced into a handle forming the medicine wheel with the four cardinal directions.
 
The workshop will span two days - 
 
Day 1:
Honor the plants and animals that gave their lives for our drums, and create a sacred place to build our drums.
The hides will be pre-soaked on-site in a pond, capturing a bit of the Wildman hidden at the bottom.
You will build your drum by stretching the rawhide over the frame and lacing it up.
 
Day  2: (one week later to give the new drums a chance to dry and tighten)
Build the drum stick.
Give the newly created drums their voice and celebrate their birth with a special ceremony.
(optional) Family potluck.
 
Each man will be responsible for the cost of a drum kit (~$70 for a 15" drum) which will be purchased in bulk from Centralia Fur & Hide . We will do a group order to save on shipping costs.
